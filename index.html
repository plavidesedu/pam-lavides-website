About

I'm Pam — an online English tutor from the Philippines who has been helping students build confidence in English since 2015. I work with learners across Asia, especially from Taiwan, Japan, China, and Korea, focusing on natural communication, pronunciation, and real-world English.

My passion for English started early. I grew up paying close attention to pronunciation and grammar, then spent years improving my own speaking by mimicking movies and recording my voice. That experience shaped the way I teach today: with patience, encouragement, and practical guidance.

Whether you're preparing for an interview, improving your conversational skills, or sounding more natural in everyday English, I'll help you understand not just what to say, but why native speakers say it that way.
