About

Hi there! I'm Pam Lavides, coming to you from the vibrant islands of the Philippines. I've been an English tutor since 2015, and honestly? I love it now more than ever.

Teaching English has been a truly fulfilling experience for me, as it not only allows me to help my students improve their language skills but also allows me to learn about different cultures from around the world. I take great pride in being nurturing and supportive, ensuring that each of my students feels comfortable and confident in their learning journey.

My journey with English started at home. I grew up with a mother who was meticulous about pronunciation and the "little things" — like getting those tricky articles (a, an, the) just right. Even so, it wasn't always "smooth sailing." I remember watching Hollywood films as a kid and feeling lost while my family laughed at the jokes. That language barrier is exactly why I do what I do today. I knew then that I didn't just want to understand English; I wanted to master its rhythm, its intonation, and its soul. I spent years mimicking actors in the mirror and recording my own voice to perfect my intonation.

Today, I use that same passion to help students from all over Asia — especially my students in Taiwan, Japan, China, and Korea — find their own confident voice. Whether you want to sound more natural or need to bridge the gap between textbook English and the real world, I'm here to guide you. I look forward to welcoming you to my class and helping you achieve your English language goals!
