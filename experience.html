My Professional Timeline

  • 2015 – 2018 · Building Strong Foundations

    Learning to teach every kind of learner

    I began my online teaching career in 2015 with one of the largest online English schools. During these early years, I taught students of all ages — from energetic 4-year-olds to professionals and retirees in their 80s. I also worked as a Senior Teacher and an Emergency Substitute Teacher, stepping into classes with little notice and adapting quickly to different students, learning styles, and lesson objectives.

    As my experience grew, I joined other online platforms, including RareJob and Bizmates, where I started working more closely with adult learners and business professionals. These years shaped my teaching philosophy and taught me how to explain English in a simple, practical, and encouraging way.

  • 2018 – 2020 · Expanding Across Global Platforms

    Helping professionals communicate with confidence

    I continued teaching through multiple international platforms, including AmazingTalker and RareJob's Business English program. During this period, I worked with students from Japan, Taiwan, South Korea, Thailand, Malaysia, Portugal, and many other countries.

    Many of my students were engineers, managers, professors, doctors, researchers, flight attendants, and other working professionals who wanted to improve their communication for meetings, presentations, interviews, and everyday conversations. Teaching learners from different cultures gave me a deeper understanding of the unique challenges speakers of different languages face when learning English.

  • 2020 – Present · Personalized Teaching & Curriculum Design

    Creating lessons that match every student's goals

    In 2020, I began accepting private students alongside my work with online schools. This gave me the freedom to move beyond standardized lesson materials and design lessons around each student's individual goals.

    Instead of relying on a single textbook, I carefully curate high-quality learning resources from trusted online sources, create my own IELTS speaking practice activities, and develop customized job interview preparation lessons tailored to each student's profession, experience, and career goals.

    Whether a student is preparing for an important interview, improving workplace communication, studying for IELTS, or simply becoming a more confident English speaker, every lesson is designed to be practical, engaging, and directly relevant to real-life situations.

Heritage

The Filipino Advantage: A Heritage of English

Many students ask why English is so prevalent in the Philippines. My teaching is backed by a deep-rooted historical connection to the language.

Did you know?

The United States established a free public school system in the Philippines in 1901, using English as the sole medium of instruction. For nearly 50 years, American educators — known as the "Thomasites" — built the foundation of our education system.

Because of this history, English is not an "alien" language to me; it is my second language. I grew up in a system where textbooks, lectures, and government proceedings were all in English. This near-native environment allows me to offer you the best of both worlds: the fluency of a native speaker and the patience of a teacher who understands the mechanics of learning English as a second language.
