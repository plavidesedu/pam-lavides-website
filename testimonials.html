Feedback

Dear Pam, I just wanted to share some good news with you. I received an offer! Thank you so much for all your guidance and support throughout this journey. I truly appreciate all your support throughout the interview prep process — guidance, encouragement, and thoughtful edits made a huge difference. I felt much more confident going into each round, and I'm really grateful for your help in polishing not just my answers, but also my mindset. Also, I'd love to continue taking your English classes!

— Wayne Lin, Industrial Designer Manager

I could speak fluent English when I was in high school. However, I didn't have a chance to speak English for over 10 years after I graduated. About one year ago, I had to speak English with my client but I found out that my English had already become very rusty! I was very disappointed. Since then, I have been practicing my speaking skills on AmazingTalker. One year passed and I feel more confident in speaking English now. Practice makes perfect. Pam is one of my teachers. She is almost ten years younger than me. She is fun and energetic. I enjoyed talking with her. Once she had a chance to visit my country, she gathered all her students in my country together to have a meal at a restaurant. Almost 20 people came to meet her! She definitely is a good teacher, because so many students wanted to meet her. We had an enjoyable night, and I still remember the happy atmosphere during the meal. It's hard to meet a good teacher. I'm lucky that I found mine. Good luck to all of you.

— Yuki Sio, Eye Doctor

從國小一年級我就開始接觸英文了，但是每當要開始上課的時候心情就會變得很低落，一想到要考試就不想去上英文了。但是自從爸爸幫我找了Pam老師後，我的心情開始變的不一樣了，每當要上課前我的心情總是既期待又開心的。上課的時候老師也很幽默風趣，讓課堂變的很有趣，雖然課堂上老師也會有一些小測試，但是我一點也不會緊張。在每次開始上課之前老師都會幫我複習上一次比較不會唸的單字，在增加單字量的同時也可以有和外國人講話的機會，一舉兩得。

— Doris, Junior High Student
English Translation I've been learning English since first grade, but whenever class was about to start, I would feel down — thinking about tests made me not want to go to English class. But ever since my dad found Teacher Pam for me, my feelings changed. Before every class, I always feel both excited and happy. During lessons, the teacher is also very funny and lively, which makes the class really fun. Even though there are small quizzes in class, I don't feel nervous at all. Before each lesson starts, the teacher always helps me review words I had trouble pronouncing last time. While building my vocabulary, I also get the chance to talk with a foreigner — two birds with one stone.
