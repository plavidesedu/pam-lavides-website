I hadn’t used English for more than 10 years because my previous job gave me very few chances to speak it. After I moved to Singapore for work, I realized how much my English had declined and how important it is to be able to speak English fluently. I moved back to Taiwan last year, and I realized that I need to keep myself in an English-speaking environment. A friend of mine who joined Pam’s class recommended it to me. After I finished the first trial class, I decided to join Pam’s class immediately. Pam is very kind and creates a no-pressure learning environment. We chat like friends during class, which makes learning English feel natural and comfortable. She always corrects my sentences in a warm and encouraging way. That’s why I truly enjoy every class with her.

— Iling, Executive Assistant
